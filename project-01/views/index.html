Welcome to Book Exchange

About Book Exchange


Hi! Welcome to Book Exchange! This is a simple online platform for the nostalgic tyoe too swap books and share each others' favourites!


How This Works


"I wanna put my book out there!"

  • Click on the "Activate Exchange" button to begin the process
  • Fill out the exchange proposal form with whichever book you would like to share
  • Click "Save Exchange"!

It's that simple! Once you've submitted the form, your book and details will appear in the Open Exchanges section!



"Someone proposed a book I like, how do I pick that?"

  • Click the "I want dis" checkbox under the book information; this should make the info change from [green] to [red]

Yup! You're all done! Now you can contact whoever the book belongs to and start exchanging!
