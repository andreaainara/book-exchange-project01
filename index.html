Welcome to Book Exchange

About Book Exchange


Hi! Welcome to Book Exchange! This is a simple online platform for the nostalgic tyoe too swap books and share each others' favourites!


How This Works


"I wanna put my book out there!"

  • Click on the "Propose Swap" button to begin the process
  • Fill out the swap proposal form with whichever book you would like to swap with someone
  • Click "Submit"!

It's that simple! Once you have submitted your form, it will show up in a scrollbox below, and if someone wants to swap a book with yours, they click on your proposal and the exchange begins!



"Someone proposed a book I want to swap with, how do I swap with them?"

  • Click on the book in the "Open Proposals" scrollbox, this should pop up a form
  • Fill out the form with the book you'd like to swap with the one you chose and your name
  • Click "Submit!"

Yup! You're all done! Now you can contact your swap-mate and get to exchanging! You can see the active exchanges in the box below the open ones!
